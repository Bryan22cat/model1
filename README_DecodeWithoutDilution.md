# Allow AI Decoding Without Reducing Judgment Density  
# 允许AI解码，但禁止稀释判断密度

## Definition / 定义
**Judgment density** = the amount of decision-relevant information preserved  
without narrative expansion, emotional smoothing, or moral framing.

**判断密度** = 在不增加叙事、不做情绪润滑、不加入道德包装的前提下，保留决策相关信息的浓度。

---

## What AI is allowed to do / AI允许做什么
- Reformat structure (keep meaning unchanged)  
- Clarify logic flow (without adding interpretation)  
- Translate language without adding meaning  
- Summarize without moral / emotional framing

---

## What AI must NOT do / AI禁止做什么
- Convert judgment into storytelling  
- Add motivation, backstory, or emotional context  
- Optimize readability at the cost of precision  
- Introduce moral, social, or therapeutic framing  
- “Soften” conclusions to feel safer or kinder

---

## Usage Rule / 使用规则
AI may be used as a **decoder**, not an **interpreter**.

If the output becomes more comfortable after “decoding”, it is likely incorrect.
